Paws for Pals

Paws for Pals is a humane animal shelter dedicated to the rescure of lost, injured and abandoned animals.

Animal shelters are faced with the issue of wanting to open their arms to more animals but not having the means to do so.

Paws for Pals is a small, local shelter in Orange County, California that opened to combat the rising numbers of animals in need of shelter.

We are a non-kill shelter that takes in all domestic animals from cats, dogs, birds, bunnies, reptiles, etc.

You can reach us at our office (714) Paw-Pals or (714) 729-7257.

You can contact a fellow member through email at paws for pals@gmail.com.

We hope to be in touch with you soon and potentially help bring a loving animal from our care to yours!
